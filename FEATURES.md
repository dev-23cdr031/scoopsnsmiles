# Sakthi Bakers Website - Complete Feature List

## Core Pages
- **Home Page** - Hero section, featured products, promotions, loyalty program, testimonials, recipes, newsletter
- **Products Page** - Full product catalog with category filtering and search functionality
- **Product Details Page** - Individual product pages with related items, ratings, and descriptions
- **About Page** - Company story, team profiles, core values, delivery information, statistics
- **Contact Page** - Contact form, location info, hours, and custom order inquiries
- **Gift Cards Page** - Gift card options with pricing and interactive selection

- **Color Palette**: Warm cream background, deep brown primary, soft gold accents
